# LCCU FinX — Guardian User Guide

**App:** LCCU FinX  
**Provided by:** Laborie Co-operative Credit Union Ltd  
**Role:** Guardian (Parent / Legal Guardian)

---

## What is LCCU FinX?

LCCU FinX is a school-based savings app that lets you monitor your child / children's account(s), review withdrawal requests they have submitted, and approve or decline those requests. You can also view a full transaction history for their records.

---

## Signing In

1. Open the LCCU FinX app.
2. Enter your registered **email address** and **password**.
3. Tap **Sign In**.

> Your account is created by the administrator. Contact the school office if you have not received your credentials.

### Forgot Your Password

1. Tap **Forgot Password** on the sign-in screen.
2. Enter your email and tap **Send Code**.
3. Enter the verification code sent to your email.
4. Set a new password.

---

## Your Home Screen

After signing in you will see the **Guardian Home** screen.

### Children Overview Table

A table at the top lists every child linked to your account with:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Child** | Your child's name |
| **Balance** | Current account balance |
| **Requests** | Number of pending withdrawal requests (tappable) |

Tap the **Requests** count to jump straight to any pending request.

---

## Reviewing a Withdrawal Request

When a child submits a withdrawal request it appears in the **Withdrawal Request** panel below the children table. It shows:

- **Time** — when the request was submitted
- **Status** — current state (Pending, Approved, Declined)
- **Amount** — how much is being requested
- **Note** — the reason the child provided for the request

You may add your own **note** in the text field before responding.

### Approving a Request

1. Read the request details carefully.
2. Optionally type a note in the note field.
3. Tap the **Approve** button (green).

The status changes to **Approved** and the teacher will process it at school.

### Declining a Request

1. Optionally explain why in the note field.
2. Tap the **Decline** button (red).

The status changes to **Declined** and the child will see the request was declined or be informed by the teacher.

---

## Viewing Transaction History

Tap the yellow **Transaction History** button to see a detailed record of all transactions across all your linked children. You can:

- Filter by child
- See dates, amounts, and transaction types

---

## Settings

Tap the **Settings** icon (top-right corner) to:

- View your registered email and role
- Read the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use
- Sign out

---

## Signing Out

**Settings** → scroll to bottom → **Sign Out**.

## Getting Help

| Issue | Contact |
|---|---|
| Incorrect child balance | Schoolthrift teacher |
| Can't sign in | App Support (Use the Contact Support link in the app (**Settings**, scroll to the Support section → tap **Contact Support**.)) |
| Request not appearing after child submitted | Ask child to check their app; contact Schoolthrift teacher |
| Transaction dispute | Schoolthrift teacher or school office|
