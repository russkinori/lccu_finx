# LCCU FinX — Student User Guide

**App:** LCCU FinX  
**Provided by:** Laborie Co-operative Credit Union Ltd  
**Role:** Student

---

## What is LCCU FinX?

LCCU FinX is a school-based savings app that lets you check your account balance, view every transaction made on your behalf, and request a withdrawal when you need to access your funds under special circumstances.

---

## Signing In

1. Open the LCCU FinX app.
2. Enter the **email address** and **password** given to you by your school or guardian.
3. Tap **Sign In**.

> If you cannot sign in, contact your teacher or the school office — account creation is handled by the administrator.

### Forgot Your Password

1. On the sign-in screen tap **Forgot Password**.
2. Enter your registered email address and tap **Send Code**.
3. Check your email for a verification code.
4. Enter the code when prompted, then set your new password.

### First-Time Use — Privacy Consent

The first time you sign in, the app shows a **Privacy & Terms** screen summarising what data LCCU holds about you and why. You must:

1. Review the summary (tap **Privacy Policy** or **Terms of Use** to read the full documents).
2. Tick the acknowledgement checkbox.
3. Tap **Accept & Continue**.

This screen only appears once. On all future sign-ins you go straight to your home screen.

---

## Your Home Screen

After signing in you will land on the **Student Home** screen. From top to bottom it contains:

| Section | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Welcome header** | Your name |
| **Account Balance pill** | Your current account balance |
| **Transaction History card** | A list of all deposits and withdrawals on your account |
| **Withdrawal Request card** | The status of your most recent withdrawal request |
| **Request Withdrawal button** | Opens the withdrawal request form |

---

## Viewing Your Transaction History

The **Transaction History** card shows every transaction on your account:

- **Date** — when the transaction was posted
- **Transaction** — type (e.g. Deposit, Withdrawal)
- **Amount** — positive for deposits, negative for withdrawals

Scroll within the card to see older entries.

---

## Requesting a Withdrawal

1. Tap the yellow **Request Withdrawal** button at the bottom of the home screen.
2. Enter the **amount** you wish to withdraw.
3. Add an **note** explaining the reason for the withdrawal.
4. Tap **Submit**.

Your request will appear in the **Withdrawal Request** card as **Pending** until your guardian approves or declines it.

> You can only have one pending withdrawal request at a time.

---

## Understanding Request Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Waiting for guardian approval |
| **Approved** | Your guardian approved it — it will be processed by your teacher |
| **Declined** | Your guardian did not approve this request |
| **Completed** | The teacher has given you the requested amount |

---

## Notifications

Tap the **bell icon** in the top-right of the app bar to open your **Notifications inbox**. You will be notified when:

- Your guardian approves or declines a withdrawal request you submitted
- A teacher marks your approved withdrawal as completed

Unread notifications are highlighted in blue and show a red count badge on the bell. Tap any notification to mark it as read. Tap **Mark all read** at the top of the screen to clear all at once.

---

## Settings

Tap the **Settings** icon (top-right of any screen) to:

- View your registered email address and role
- Read the Privacy Policy
- Read the Terms of Use
- Sign out

---

## Signing Out

Tap the **Sign Out** button at the bottom right of the screen. 
If you're in **Settings**, scroll to the bottom → tap **Sign Out**.

## Your Privacy

LCCU FinX collects only the information needed to run your savings account:

| What we hold | Why |
|---|---|
| Your name and email | Account identification and login |
| Your school and class | Scoping your account to the right institution |
| Your account balance and transactions | Running your savings account |
| Your guardian's name | Linking your account for oversight |

We do **not** collect your date of birth, government ID, location, or any information not listed above.

Your account data is only visible to you, your linked guardian, your teacher (class-level totals), your principal (school-level totals), and authorised LCCU administrators. No one else can see your account.

If you or your guardian want to see a copy of your data, have something corrected, or close your account, contact the school office or LCCU (see Settings → Contact Support).

---

## Getting Help

Contact your **Schoolthrift teacher** or the **school office** for:
- Account balance questions
- Transaction disputes

Use the Contact Support link in the app (**Settings**, scroll to the Support section → tap **Contact Support**.) for
- Problems signing in
- Problems with the app's functionality
