# LCCU FinX — Principal User Guide

**App:** LCCU FinX  
**Provided by:** Laborie Co-operative Credit Union Ltd  
**Role:** Principal

---

## What is LCCU FinX?

LCCU FinX gives school principals a consolidated view of all teacher collections and school account activity. Your role is to reconcile the physical cash collected by teachers each week, verify the amounts, and submit a deposit batch to the credit union teller for final processing.

---

## Signing In

1. Open the LCCU FinX app.
2. Enter your registered **email address** and **password**.
3. Tap **Sign In**.

> Contact your system administrator if you have not yet received your credentials.

### Forgot Your Password

1. Tap **Forgot Password** on the sign-in screen.
2. Enter your email and tap **Send Code**.
3. Enter the verification code from your email, then set a new password.

## Your Home Screen

| Section | Description |
|---|---|
| **Welcome header** | Your name |
| **Account Balance** | Total credit union account balance for your school |
| **Funds On-Site** | Cash currently held at the school (collected but not yet deposited) |
| **Deposited Funds** | Total amount already deposited and confirmed by the credit union teller |
| **School Deposit Details** | Summary: Deposit Due / Deposited / Difference for the whole school |
| **Teacher dropdown** | Filter deposit details to a specific teacher |
| **Teacher Deposit Details** | That teacher's Deposit Due / Deposited / Difference |
| **Transaction History button** | Opens student transaction records |

---

## Understanding the Deposit Figures

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Deposit Due** | Amount expected based on student contributions |
| **Deposited** | Amount already confirmed at the credit union |
| **Difference** | Due minus Deposited — a negative figure means a shortfall |

Any difference shown in **red** indicates an outstanding amount not yet deposited.

---

## Viewing Transaction History

Tap the yellow **Transaction History** button to open the full dashboard.

- Filter records by **Teacher**, **Class**, **Student** or **Period** using the dropdowns.
- Each row shows: Date, Teacher who recorded the transaction, Transaction type, Amount.
- Tap **Export** (top-right) to download records as a CSV file for your records or the school's finance office.

---

## Reconciling Weekly Collections (Deposit Reconciliation)

At the end of each week, after teachers have handed in their collected cash:

1. From the home screen tap **Reconcile Deposit**.
2. The **Reconciliation screen** opens showing each teacher's collection for the current week.
3. Review the list of teachers and the amount each has collected.
4. Count the physical cash you have received from all teachers.
5. If all amounts match, tap **Submit Deposit Batch**.
6. Add an optional **note** (e.g. "Week of 14 July, all balanced") and confirm.

The deposit batch is sent to the credit union teller as a pending deposit. Once the teller confirms receipt of the physical cash and records the deposit, the **Deposited Funds** figure on your home screen will update.

### What if the totals do not match?

- Check with the individual teacher whose amount differs.
- You can add a note to the batch explaining any discrepancy before submitting.
- The teller will record the discrepancy when confirming the deposit.

## Monitoring Individual Teachers

1. The **Teacher Deposit Details** card updates to show that teacher's Deposit Due, Deposited, and Difference.
2. Tap the card for a history of the credit union deposits made by all teachers.

## Weekly Workflow Summary

| Day | Action |
|---|---|
| **Mon–Fri** | Teachers collect savings and submit deposits in the app |
| **End of week** | Teachers hand physical cash to you |
| **Reconciliation day** | Open Reconcile screen, verify totals match, submit batch |
| **Next business day** | Teller confirms at credit union; school balances update |
