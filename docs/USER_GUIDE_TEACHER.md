# LCCU FinX — Teacher User Guide

**App:** LCCU FinX  
**Provided by:** Laborie Co-operative Credit Union Ltd  
**Role:** Teacher

---

## What is LCCU FinX?

LCCU FinX is a school-based savings app that allows teachers to collect weekly student savings, track funds held in-hand, monitor individual student account balances, manage approved withdrawal requests, and submit deposits to the student's account.

---

## Signing In

1. Open the LCCU FinX app.
2. Enter your registered **email address** and **password**.
3. Tap **Sign In**.

> Your credentials are issued by your school administrator.

### Forgot Your Password

1. Tap **Forgot Password** on the sign-in screen.
2. Enter your email and tap **Send Code**.
3. Enter the code emailed to you and set a new password.

## Collecting Savings — The Weekly Cycle

Each week students bring cash savings to you. The typical workflow is:

1. Collect cash from students.
2. Record each student's contribution via **Submit Deposit** (see below).
3. Hand all collected cash to the **Principal** for consolidation at the end of the week.
4. The principal reconciles the amount and submits the batch for deposit.
5. The batch amount is taken to the credit union for deposit
6. The teller confirms receipt of the amount and records the deposit to the school account.

---

## Submitting a Deposit

1. From the home screen tap the yellow **Submit Deposit** button.
2. The deposit form opens showing:
   - A student selector if you are depositing on behalf of a specific student
3. Enter the deposit **amount**.
4. Optionally add a **note**.
5. Tap **Submit**.
6. Clarify the amount. 
7. Tap **Confirm** or tap **Cancel** to correct the amount. 

> After submission the amount will be reflected in the **Funds In-Hand** figure and the student's account balance and transactions.

---

## Viewing Transaction History

Tap the yellow **Transactions** button to open the **Transaction History** screen.

- Filter by **Class** or individual **Student** using the dropdowns at the top.
- Each row shows: Date, Transaction type, Amount.

---

## Handling Student Withdrawal Requests

When a student submits a withdrawal request and their guardian approves it, the request appears in your **Withdrawal Request** card with the status **Approved**.

To process an approved withdrawal:
1. Tap the request card to view the details.
2. Disburse the cash amount to the student in person.
3. Tap the specific request to mark it as completed inside the dashboard view.

> After completion the amount will be reflected in the **Funds In-Hand** figure and the student's account balance and transactions.

> **Important:** Only dispense cash for requests with status **Approved**. Pending requests still require guardian approval.

## Monitoring Student Balances

On the home or transaction screen:
- Use the **Class** dropdown to see aggregate totals for a class.
- Use the **Student** dropdown to focus on one student.
- The **Account Balance** pill updates to reflect the selected scope.

## End-of-Week Checklist

| Step | Action |
|---|---|
| 1 | Confirm all collected cash is entered via Submit Deposit |
| 2 | Verify **Funds In-Hand** matches physical cash in your possession |
| 3 | Hand cash to the Principal for reconciliation |
| 4 | Resolve any outstanding approved withdrawal requests before submitting deposits |

---

## Data Privacy Responsibilities

As a teacher you access student financial data as part of your role. You are responsible for:

- Keeping your login credentials confidential — do not share your account
- Only accessing data for students in your assigned class
- Not exporting or sharing student transaction data outside of official school or LCCU reporting channels
- Reporting any suspected unauthorised access or data issue to your school administrator immediately

Student data in LCCU FinX is used solely for the school savings programme. It is never used for marketing or shared with third parties for commercial purposes.
